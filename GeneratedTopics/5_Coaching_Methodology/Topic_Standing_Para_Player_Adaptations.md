# Standing Para-Player Adaptations

Adapting movement patterns, lunging, and balance recovery for SL3, SL4, and SU5 players.

## Structural Outline

1. SL3/SL4 adaptations: Modifying footwork patterns (e.g., hopping, step-close-step) to compensate for lower limb impairments.
2. SU5 adaptations: Balancing the body and counterweight during lunges without full use of both arms.
3. Optimizing racket grip and swing trajectories to maximize range of motion.
