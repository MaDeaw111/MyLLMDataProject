During court travel, managing decelerating forces, as seen in **Cognitive phase coaching focus: static positioning and simple, repetitive footwork drills.**, protects joints from high impact. The heel-to-toe lunge landing, with the knee aligned directly over the ankle, prevents patellar tendinopathy. Coaches must focus on correct foot alignment during rapid direction changes.
